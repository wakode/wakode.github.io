Rohit Wakode

I am a Chemical Engineering (B.Tech.) graduated from IIT Bombay in 2015 wherein I pursued part of my studies at the Technical University of Denmark.

Currently, I work as a Research Assistant at IIT Bombay pursuing research integrating my expertise in the Energy Conversion and Storage Area with rapidly growing Machine Learning tools. Previously, in addition to IIT Bombay, I worked at UC Merced, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (USA), Riso National Laboratory (Denmark), Technical University of Denmark (Denmark), Monash University (Australia).

My research interests primarily lie in the Energy Conversion and Storage Area (Lithium Ion Battery and Fuel Cells).
