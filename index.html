Rohit Wakode


Hi! Welcome to my webpage. I am a Chemical Engineer (B.Tech.) graduated from IIT Bombay in 2015 (Among top 5 out of 882 students to be felicitated with certificate and award by Dean SA of IIT Bombay for my outstanding contribution to the Institute) wherein I pursued part of my studies (MSc. courses) at the Technical University of Denmark (Among top 2 in India and top ~25 worldwide to bag the prestigious Novozymes-Holck-Larsen Fellowship 2014).

Currently, I work as a Research Assistant at IIT Bombay pursuing research integrating my expertise in the Energy Conversion and Storage Area with rapidly growing Machine Learning tools. Previously, in addition to IIT Bombay, I worked at UC Merced, Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (USA), Riso National Laboratory (Denmark), Technical University of Denmark (Denmark), Monash University (Australia).

My research interests primarily lie in the Energy Conversion and Storage Area (Lithium Ion Battery and Fuel Cells). Please refer my detailed CV here for more information.
